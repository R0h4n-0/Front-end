PRINCESS MONONOKE




Descsription:


Date: July 12, 1997
Director: Hayao Miyazaki
Screenplay: Hayao Miyazalki




The story is set in medieval Japan, and revolves around a young prince named Ashitaka who becomes cursed after defending his village from a rampaging boar god. In order to find a cure for his curse, Ashitaka sets out on a journey to the forests of the west, where he encounters a conflict between the human inhabitants of a nearby iron mining village and the spirits of the forest, led by a fierce warrior princess named San, who is known as Princess Mononoke. The mining village is led by Lady Okkoto, a giant boar god who seeks to protect her own kind and preserve their way of life. However, the village's leader, Lady Oshkii, is determined to clear the forest and mine the iron, leading to a violent clash with the forest spirits. Ashitaka attempts to broker a peace between the two sides, but is caught in the middle of their conflict. As Ashitaka and San begin to understand each other and their respective points of view, they work together to find a way to end the conflict and preserve the balance of nature. Along the way, they face opposition from Lady Oshkii and her forces, as well as from the enigmatic Lady Eboshi, the leader of the ironworks who seeks to kill the forest spirit and rule over the land. The conflict eventually reaches a climax when Lady Okkoto is killed by Lady Eboshi, and the forest spirits launch a final assault on the ironworks. Ashitaka manages to save Lady Eboshi from the spirits, but not before San is mortally wounded. In a poignant moment, Ashitaka convinces the forest spirit to restore San's life, and the two lovers watch as the spirit brings life back to the land. In the end, Lady Eboshi and her forces leave the forest and the remaining humans vow to live in harmony with nature, while Ashitaka and San remain in the forest as protectors of the land and its spirits. The film ends with the implication that the balance between humans and nature will be maintained, but that there will always be struggles to protect it.
